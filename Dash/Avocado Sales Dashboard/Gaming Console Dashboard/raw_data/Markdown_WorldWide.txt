The overall gaming console market share is dominated by PlayStation. Even the market share between Xbox and PlayStation is evenly shared in the Western world, but the PlayStation has a significantly larger share in Asian countries. The major reason is that there are more normal and exclusive titles available on the PlayStation platform than on the Xbox platform, especailly there are more partnership between Japanese game publishers and Sony. However, the online service provided by Microsoft has a better feedback than the service provided by Sony that help Microsoft to promote their Xbox to gamers for playing online co-op games.
